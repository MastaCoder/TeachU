Don't waste time creating tests. Spend more time practicing.

TeachU makes it easy to automatically create quizzes from your notes, so you can spend more time applying your skills, all for free.

Study smarter, not harder.

Reading your notes over again have been proven through research to not be an effective way to study. Going through the material multiple times only improves some of your understanding. Having the ability to put the material learnt into practice with problems that will challenge your knowledge will improve your ability to understanding the material.

Our Mission

TeachU's objective is to improve the way we study. Instead of spending time re-reading the material or spending time searching for tests, our system allows students to jump straight into practice. Using the power of machine learning, our system finds the best way to test users straight from their own notes.
